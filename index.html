Ghana

Ghana, located in West Africa, is known for its rich cultural heritage, warm hospitality, and diverse landscapes. As home to historic castles, vibrant markets, and beautiful coastlines, Ghana represents a blend of tradition and modernity. The country's peaceful democracy and growing economy make it a beacon of stability in the region.
